Building a house in Lebanon takes four to six years — permits that stall, quotes that drift, a site you visit every weekend to argue. It doesn't have to.

The system النظام

01

Optimize.

One team, one contract, one price. Architect, structural engineer, builder and the Order of Engineers file move together — permitting is handled before you sign, not discovered after.

02

Productize.

Each BAYT is a finished product, not a blank page. Engineered once, priced once, approved once — then adapted to your plot, your orientation, your slope. No redesign, no surprises.

03

Localize.

Panels fabricated off-site with Lebanese workshops — stone from Batroun quarries, joinery from Tripoli, furniture from Beirut ateliers. Assembled on your land in weeks, not seasons.

The process الطريقة

  1. 01

    Choose a model

    SAHEL, JABAL or WADI — with your finishes, from a curated Lebanese palette.

  2. 02

    Plot review

    We check zoning, exploitation ratios and municipality requirements for your land. Free, within two weeks.

  3. 03

    One signature, one price

    A single fixed-price contract in USD — design, permits, construction, interior. No revisions to chase.

  4. 04

    Fabrication

    Panels, stone and joinery produced off-site by our partner workshops while permits complete.

  5. 05

    Weeks on site, not years. You watch a house arrive, not a construction saga.
